Title: startup-disk has been upstreamed
Author: James Calligeros <jcalligeros99@gmail.com>
Posted: 2024-03-03
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 2.0

The GTK4 frontend for asahi-bless, previously just called startup-disk, has
been upstreamed into ::gentoo as asahi-startup-disk. As the name of the package
has changed, you will need to manually uninstall sys-apps/startup-disk::asahi
and install sys-apps/asahi-startup-disk::gentoo in its stead.
